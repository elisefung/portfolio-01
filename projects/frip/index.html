Frip

  • UX design
  • Web development

Frip is a mobile app that builds flexible itineraries for groups of friends exploring a new city. This is my interactive media capstone project that I worked on with a small group of designers, practicing design thinking and iterating through ideas throughout our senior year. Our group imagined a better solution for planning a trip with a group of friends and used our own experiences and frustrations to brainstorm creative solutions. Through user interviews, wireframing, and usability testing, we created a prototype for a mobile app that eases the stress of planning with a group by allowing people to suggest things they want to do and anonymously vote on activities. I also developed a demo marketing website that we used to visualize our product in an innovation expo we attended.
