Trainer is a responsive web app for competitive athletes and their coaches, created for an interaction design course at Northeastern. The concept behind this project came from my friend’s frustrations in communicating with her personal trainer when she competed in a bodybuilding competition. Creating a persona around my friend’s experience, I sketched ideas, tested, iterated, and prototyped to refine my concept. I designed Trainer as a platform for competitors and coaches to manage training plans, track progress, meal plans, and workouts. Read the process documentation
